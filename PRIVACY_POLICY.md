# Privacy Policy for Capas

_Last updated: May 1, 2026_

Capas ("we", "our", or "the App") respects your privacy.  
This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

---

## Information We Collect
The App does **not collect, store, or share any personal information** from its users.  
We do not require registration, login, or access to device data.

The Android version of the App uses **Firebase Crashlytics** (provided by Google) to collect crash diagnostics. When the App crashes, Crashlytics automatically sends a crash report containing the stack trace, device model, operating-system version, and a short trail of in-app events (such as screen navigations and network calls) that occurred just before the crash. These reports help us identify and fix bugs. They do **not** include personal information, account data, or anything you have viewed inside the App.

---

## How We Use Information
Since no personal information is collected, there is no usage of such data.  
The App only displays publicly available newspaper covers from third-party sources.

Crash diagnostics collected via Firebase Crashlytics are used solely to identify and resolve technical problems in the App.

---

## Third-Party Content
The App may display content (e.g., newspaper covers) from external sources.  
We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third parties.

Crash diagnostics on Android are processed by Google Firebase. See Google's privacy policy at [policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details.

---

## Children’s Privacy
The App does not target or knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
